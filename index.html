Azadeh Khaleghi

I am a Professor of Statistics at École Nationale de la Statistique et de l'Administration Économique (ENSAE) - Centre de Recherche en Économie et Statistique (CREST). My research interests are in the mathematical foundations of Statistics and Machine Learning. My main focus is on devising nonparametric models for long-memory time series and Multi-Armed Bandits. I am equally interested in studying the possibilities and limitations of Algorithmic Fairness.
