About

I am a Professor of Statistics at ENSAE and researcher at CREST. My work focuses on rigorous nonparametric methods for dependent data and sequential decision problems. I develop theory, algorithms, and open-source software.

Available for PhD supervision and collaborations. ORCID: 0000-0001-8643-5416
