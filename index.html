About

I am a Professor of Statistics at ENSAE and CREST. My research interests are in the mathematical foundations of Statistics and Machine Learning. My main focus is on devising nonparametric models for long-memory time series and Multi-Armed Bandits.

Available for PhD supervision and collaborations. ORCID: 0000-0001-8643-5416 • Google Scholar: Profile
